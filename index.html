Who is Catamaran Dan

Catamaran Dan is a lifelong sailor, U.S. Navy submariner, and expedition skipper focused on small-boat sailing adventures built around skill, self-reliance, and authentic exploration.

This is not luxury yachting. This is hands-on sailing and learning by doing.

The Boat

Hobie Cat Getaway — Shamrock

Shamrock is a beach-launchable, trailerable catamaran designed for coastal raids, island hopping, and shallow-water access that larger boats simply can’t reach.

Small boat. Serious range.

Base Camp

Nevado Ranch Camp — Arizona

Operations are staged from Nevado Ranch Camp in Arizona’s White Mountains — an unlikely but deliberate launch point for global sailing expeditions.

  • Expedition planning
  • Boat preparation
  • Skill development
  • Route design

Crew & Partnership

Catamaran Dan is open to finding a female first mate to join him on future sailing adventures and expedition legs.

Seamanship, curiosity, adaptability, and humor matter more than résumés.

Blue Rim Five

A Global Sailing Expedition

Exploring coastlines, islands, and cultures by small boat — with purpose.

What is Blue Rim Five

Blue Rim Five is a multi-year global sailing expedition following the world’s coastlines rather than racing across open oceans.

The focus is navigation, seamanship, and connection with people and place — not speed, luxury, or shortcuts.

How it’s Done

Small, capable boats

Trailerable and shallow-draft vessels allow access where large yachts cannot go.

Coastal focus

Routes prioritize learning, exploration, and real navigation challenges.

Basecamp supported

Nevado Ranch Camp serves as the planning and reset hub between expedition legs.

Status

Blue Rim Five is in active development, with vessel preparation, shakedown sailing, and route planning underway.
