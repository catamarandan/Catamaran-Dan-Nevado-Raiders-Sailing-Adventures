A modern Viking expedition movement

Nevado Raiders™

Nevado Raiders™ is a modern Viking-spirited expedition movement for people who still feel the pull of the horizon. Forged at Nevado Ranch Camp in the high country of Arizona and proven on the water, the Raiders blend seamanship, self-reliance, and exploration with deep respect for coastlines, cultures, and a rapidly warming world.

At the center of the fleet are small expedition sailboats—Hobie Getaways, Catalina 22 Sports, Nacra 570s, and similar vessels— where real skills are learned close to the water. Guardian vessels stand watch, carrying resources and safety margin so the small boats can travel farther, smarter, and safer.

Small boats, real skill Guardian vessels Training + real miles

Raiders live by a simple code: prepare always, protect your crew, respect every coastline, own mistakes, and never stop learning. Being a Raider™ isn’t about the boat you own—it’s about how you show up for your crew, for kids and families, and for the truth of a changing planet.

Blue Rim 5™ Global Sailing Expedition

Blue Rim 5™

Blue Rim 5™ is a long-range, multi-year global sailing expedition tracing the blue edge of the planet—coastlines, islands, harbors, and river mouths reachable under sail. It moves one leg at a time with a mixed fleet of small expedition sailboats and larger guardian vessels, following a simple rule: we go where it makes sense, when it makes sense, and stop or reroute when it doesn’t.

This is not a cruise, not a yacht club, and not a packaged tour. Crews join for the legs that fit their lives, help tell the truth about each coastline, then pass the wake forward. Kids and families are central to the mission—putting young people on real shorelines so they can see, first-hand, how a dangerously warming world is already reshaping life along the waterline.

Small boats + guardian vessels Multi-year, leg-by-leg Kids & coastlines first

The model is built for the century we actually live in: start small, learn fast, build the fleet, and keep going as long as the risks are acceptable. We will not trace the planet’s rim and lie to children about what’s happening to it.

Captain • Instructor • Primary training & expedition platform

Catamaran Dan & the Hobie Getaway "Shamrocket"

Shamrocket is the Hobie Getaway that carries the reps—boat handling, anchoring, beach landings, surf etiquette, weather judgment, and disciplined routines. This is where skills get forged before bigger water and longer legs.

Catamaran Dan is a USCG Captain (200-ton) and lifelong sailing instructor (20+ years). U.S. Navy submariner—Blue Nose and Shellback— with deep experience in communications and navigation. This project is about competence, courage, and taking care of people on the water.
