Nevado Raiders™

We’re building a fleet model that is and/and: small expedition sailboats and larger guardian vessels. The spirit is Viking-influenced—shallow draft, coastal access, daily landings—paired with modern seamanship, planning, and restraint.

  • Fleet: small expedition boats + guardian vessels (sail or power).
  • Ethos: virtually anywhere on Earth—as long as it makes sense and the risks are acceptable.
  • Core: skill, self-reliance, cultural exchange, peace, and human connection.

Blue Rim 5™ Global Sailing Expedition

The expedition is built around disciplined risk management and a realistic operating model where each participating boat/owner covers their own expenses at this stage. The goal is to show what small vessels can do—safely—at a relatively small cost.

  • Launch: Alabama • October 2026
  • Model: participant vessels cover their own travel expenses (for now).
  • Safety: guardian vessels elevate the fleet’s safety margin on longer crossings.

Guardian Vessels

Guardian vessels bring higher capability, redundancy, and a safer envelope when conditions get serious—especially during longer inter-island crossings, night contingencies, and weather windows.

  • Typical: 30’+ (sailing or power), with experienced skipper/crew.
  • Role: safety presence, comms relay, contingency support, mentorship.

First Mate

We’re shaping the standards early: competence, calm decision-making, humor, and shared purpose. The kind of person who can help make a hard day feel manageable—and a good day unforgettable.

  • Core traits: seamanship, emotional steadiness, teamwork, curiosity.
  • Culture: respect, accountability, and forward momentum.

Shamrocket (Hobie Getaway)

Shallow draft and beach access aren’t gimmicks—they’re the whole point. The Getaway’s ability to land, launch, and move fast between coastal stops mirrors why longboats were so effective: access + simplicity + options.

Cultural Exchange

We’re aiming for respectful presence and honest storytelling—meeting communities on their own terms and honoring the shared responsibility to care for one another.

Rim Runner™

A fixed ritual matters. It makes the journey feel like something you can carry—anywhere on Earth—back to one simple moment: horizon, gratitude, and the crew you chose.
